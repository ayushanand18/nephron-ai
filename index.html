Nephron AI by NightLight AI


An innovative project that aims to use power of AI to predict chronic Kidney Disease as well as detect its causes and also give an important personalised feedback.

This projects uses AI (Artificial Intelligence) to predict whether a person may contract with Chronic Kidney Disease or not based on 8 Medical Attributes. And Also, gives out the possible cause for the contraction based on the results of a short quiz. This project is under development. The founder and developer of this project is 15 year old high school student at New Delhi, India.
